FAQs

How do I add a subscription?

Navigate to the Subscriptions page and click the "+" button to add new subscriptions.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Use the Export data button to generate and export detailed reports.

Does Trackify send payment reminders?

Yes, email notifications alert you about upcoming due dates and free trial expirations.

Can I track free trials?

Yes, Trackify tracks free trial end dates and sends timely reminders.

Is Trackify free to use?

Yes, Trackify is absolutely free of cost.

I am not receiving any email notifications. What should I do?

Please follow these steps: Check your Spam or Junk folder. Search for “Trackify” in your inbox. Ensure your email address was entered correctly in the form.
