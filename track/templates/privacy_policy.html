Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2025

At Trackify, we care about your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your personal information while helping you manage subscriptions with ease and transparency.

1. What Information We Collect

We collect only the data needed to provide our services:

  • Personal Info – Such as your name and email when you register.
  • Subscription Details – The services you track, including name, cost, category, and renewal dates.
  • Usage Data – General insights on how you interact with the platform to help us improve functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Help you monitor your subscriptions and set up timely reminders.
  • Provide summaries, countdowns, and insights for better budget awareness.
  • Enhance the platform based on user feedback and interaction patterns.

3. Your Privacy & Security

Your data is stored securely and treated with care:

  • We use encryption and limited access to keep your information safe.
  • Your information is never sold or shared with third parties.
  • Regular checks ensure we maintain a secure and reliable environment.

4. Your Choices & Rights

You remain in control of your data:

  • You can update or delete your subscription details at any time.
  • You can turn off reminders or unsubscribe from emails.
  • You may request permanent deletion of your data.

5. Third-Party Tools

Trackify may use trusted tools for services like email notifications or analytics, but your data is never shared unnecessarily.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we make updates, we'll inform you via email or platform notice to keep you fully informed.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy, feel free to reach us at support@trackify.com.
